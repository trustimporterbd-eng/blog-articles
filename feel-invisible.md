# Feel Invisible

Original Article:
https://shesalreadyher.com/feel-invisible/

## Summary
This article discusses the emotional experience of feeling invisible in relationships or social environments. Many people constantly give their time, care, and attention to others, but still feel unseen and unappreciated.

## Key Idea
Feeling invisible often happens when people value what you do, but fail to see who you truly are.
